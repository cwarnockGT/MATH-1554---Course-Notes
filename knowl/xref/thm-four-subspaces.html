Theorem 6.17. The Four Subspaces.

For any \(A\in\mathbb{R}^{m\times n}\text{:}\)
🔗
  • \(\Nul(A)\) is the orthogonal complement of \(\operatorname{Row}(A)\text{:}\)
    🔗
    \begin{equation*} \Row(A)^\perp = \Nul(A) \quad \text{and} \quad \Nul(A)^\perp = \Row(A) \end{equation*}
    🔗
  • \(\Nul(A^T)\) is the orthogonal complement of \(\operatorname{Col}(A)\text{:}\)
    🔗
    \begin{equation*} \Col(A)^\perp = \Nul(A^T) \quad \text{and} \quad \Nul(A^T)^\perp = \Col(A) \end{equation*}
    🔗
