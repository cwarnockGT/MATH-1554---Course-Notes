Example 7.8.

With this adjustment and with \(p=0.85\text{,}\) what is \(G\) for Example 7.4? Is it regular?
🔗
Solution.
We have that
\begin{equation*} K = \begin{bmatrix} \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \\ \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \\ \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \\ \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \\ \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \amp \frac{1}{5} \end{bmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Then
\begin{equation*} G = 0.85P_* + 0.15K\text{,} \end{equation*}
which is regular since the addition of \(K\) guarantees that every entry of \(G\) is positive.
