Definition 3.1. Determinant.

Suppose \(A\) is \(n\times n\) with entries \(a_{ij}\text{.}\) Then the determinant of \(A\text{,}\) written \(\det(A)\) or \(\det A\) or \(\left| A\right|\text{,}\) is defined by the following recursive formula.
